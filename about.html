Our Mission

Jewish Educational Media (JEM) is dedicated to preserving and sharing the teachings, wisdom, and legacy of the Rebbe, Rabbi Menachem M. Schneerson, of righteous memory. Through state-of-the-art restoration, digitization, and distribution, we ensure that future generations can experience the Rebbe's profound impact on Jewish life and beyond.

Our Journey

Four decades of dedication to preserving history

1980

The Beginning

JEM was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to document and preserve the Rebbe's every public appearance, teaching, and interaction for posterity.

1995

Digital Revolution

We began the massive undertaking of digitizing decades of analog footage, making it searchable and accessible to a global audience.

2010

HD Restoration

Launched our state-of-the-art restoration studio, bringing rare footage from the 1950s-1990s into stunning high definition.

2024

Global Platform

Launched our modern streaming platform, making the complete archive accessible worldwide with advanced search, translations, and community features.
